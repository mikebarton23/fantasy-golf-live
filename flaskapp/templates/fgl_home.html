Welcome to Fantasy Golf Live!

Rules of the Game

Each week, you will select six golfers to compete on your team. Log in and draft your team by Wednesday night at 11:59 PM to be eligible to compete. Once your team has been selected, the fun begins. Your team's score is calculated based on your best four players at any given time (i.e. if you have four players that are playing well and two that aren't, only your top four count). But wait, there's more! If three (or more) of your players fail to make the cut, you will be eliminated from contention. Draft your team today and enjoy the fun!
